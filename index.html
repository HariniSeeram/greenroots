Organic Farming & Plantation in Palakol

This website is part of my Community Service Project on Organic Farming & Plantation in Palakol Town, West Godavari. The goal is to spread awareness about growing vegetables at home using native seeds and simple composting methods.

  • Grow safe, chemical-free vegetables at home
  • Use kitchen waste to make natural compost
  • Support local nurseries and farmers

Project Snapshot

Location: Palakol Town, West Godavari

Duration: 05/05/2025 – 05/07/2025

Focus: Organic farming, native seeds, composting
